Interview Rules

1. Start Button: Begin the interview by clicking on the start button.

2. Time Limit: Each question has a maximum answering time of 30 seconds. If the time exceeds, move to the next question.

3. Webcam On: Keep your webcam turned on throughout the interview session.

4. AI Avatar: An AI avatar will ask each question one by one.

5. Answer Order: Answer questions in the same order they are presented.

6. Next Button: Click on the next button to move to the next question after providing your answer.

7. No Interruptions: Avoid interruptions or distractions during the interview.

8. Honesty: Provide honest and concise answers to each question.

9. Feedback: Once all questions are answered, provide feedback if prompted.

10. Professionalism: Maintain a professional demeanor throughout the interview process.
